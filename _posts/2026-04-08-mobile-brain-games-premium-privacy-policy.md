# Privacy Policy & Terms of Service  

**Konstantinos Thelouras** (also referred to as “we”, “us”, or “our”) built the **Mobile Brain Games Premium** app (the “Service”) as a commercial, freemium application for Android and iOS. This page informs you about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when anyone decides to use the Service, and sets out the terms that govern your use of the Service.

---  

## Privacy Policy  

### 1. Information We Collect  

| Source | What Is Collected | Where It Is Stored | Purpose |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|---------|
| **Local device storage** (SharedPreferences) | • User profile data (`userId`, `joinDate`, `premiumStatus`, streaks, total games, play time, per‑game stats, high scores, best reaction times, etc.)<br>• Selected locale<br>• Demo‑game‑play tracking flags | On the user’s device only | Persist app state, enable offline play, remember language preference |
| **Firebase Anonymous Authentication** | Anonymous UID (generated by Firebase Auth) | Firebase Auth (cloud) | Provide a stable identifier for syncing the local profile across devices |
| **Cloud Firestore** (via `SyncService`) | Full user profile (same fields as above) and a history of game results (`gameId`, `category`, `score`, `duration`, `metadata`) | `users/{uid}` collection & `users/{uid}/history` sub‑collection | Backup user progress, enable cross‑device sync, allow us to restore data if the user reinstalls the app |
| **Firebase Analytics** | Event logs such as `app_open`, `screen_view`, `game_start`, `game_complete`, `paywall_view`, `purchase_attempt`, `purchase_success`, `purchase_failure`, `ui_interaction` plus user properties `is_premium`, `total_games_played`, `current_streak`, `best_streak` | Google’s analytics servers (Google Analytics for Firebase) | Understand how the Service is used, monitor app health, improve UX, and measure conversion to the pro version |
| **RevenueCat** (one‑time purchase manager) | Transaction identifier, receipt data, anonymous device identifier, purchase status (entitlement “Mobile Brain Games Premium Pro”) | RevenueCat servers | Verify that the user has performed the one‑time purchase and to unlock the pro features |
| **Error / Crash Data** (optional – only when a crash occurs) | Stack traces, error messages, device model, OS version, app version, battery level, network type, locale, timestamp, synthetic device identifier | Sent to Firebase (if Crashlytics is enabled in the future) | Diagnose and fix technical issues |

**What We Do Not Collect**  
We do **not** collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or location. No advertising SDKs (AdMob, Facebook, etc.) are included, and we never request camera, microphone, contacts, or health data.

### 2. How We Use Your Information  

- **Provide & improve the Service** – store game progress, sync it to the cloud, and restore it on new devices.  
- **Enable pro features** – verify your one‑time purchase status via RevenueCat.  
- **Analytics & performance monitoring** – aggregate usage data (no personal identifiers) to guide feature development.  
- **Error reporting** – optional crash logs help us resolve bugs.  

All data is processed in accordance with the privacy policies of the third‑party services listed below.

### 3. Third‑Party Services  

| Service | What They May Collect | Links to Their Policies |
|---------|----------------------|--------------------------|
| **Firebase (Google Analytics, Auth, Firestore)** | Event data, anonymous UID, stored documents | <https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy> |
| **RevenueCat** | Purchase receipt, device identifier, entitlement status | <https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy> |
| **Google Play Billing (Android)** | Transaction IDs, payment method (handled by Google) | <https://play.google.com/about/privacy/> |
| **Apple App Store (iOS)** | Transaction IDs, receipt data (handled by Apple) | <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/> |

We do not share your data with any other parties, except as required to process payments (Apple/Google) or to operate the above services.

### 4. One‑Time Purchase & Pro Unlock  

- The Service uses a **freemium** model. Five games are free; nine additional games become permanently available after a **single, lifetime purchase** (“Mobile Brain Games Premium Pro”).  
- The purchase is managed through **RevenueCat** as a **one‑time entitlement**, not a subscription.  
- When you make the purchase, RevenueCat records the transaction identifier and receipt, validates it with the corresponding store (Google Play or Apple App Store), and grants you the “Mobile Brain Games Premium Pro” entitlement forever.  
- No recurring billing, auto‑renewal, or future charges occur.

### 5. Permissions  

| Platform | Permission | Reason |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Android | `android.permission.INTERNET` | Required for all network communication (Firebase, RevenueCat, sync). |
| Android | `android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE` | Detect connectivity to enable/disable online features. |
| Android | `com.android.vending.BILLING` | Required for Google Play one‑time purchase. |
| iOS | No special permissions declared. | The app does not request camera, microphone, location, contacts, etc. |

### 6. Security  

We employ industry‑standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and rely on the security measures of Firebase and RevenueCat for data at rest. While we strive to protect your information, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100 % secure.

### 7. Children’s Privacy  

The Service is **not directed at children under 13** years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone in that age group. If you become aware that a child under 13 has inadvertently provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.

### 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy  

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised version in the app and update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

### 9. Contact Information  

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the email address provided in the app‑store listing or through the “Contact” link on the official website.
