# Privacy Policy & Terms of Service 

Konstantinos Thelouras built the app called **Odd One Out**.

### Data Collection & Privacy
This application is designed with privacy as a priority, especially considering its target audience. 
* **No Analytics:** The app does not use any third-party analytics or tracking software.
* **No Data Collection:** No personal information or gameplay data is collected or transmitted to external servers.
* **Local Storage:** Best scores and game history are stored locally on your device only.
* **No Data Sharing:** This app does not share any user data with third parties.

### Technical Stack
The app is built using the following technologies:
- **Flutter**
- **Dart**

### Libraries Used
- `cupertino_icons`: ^1.0.2
- `shared_preferences`: (Used for saving local high scores and accessibility settings)
- `provider`: (Used for state management)
